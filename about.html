About Me

IT'S ALL ABOUT MY STORY

I have been a passionate make up artist

Driven by vision and inspired by beauty, Michelle Patricio's work reflects both innovation and style. Paired with her natural ability to enhance, manipulate or beautify; Michelle graduated in Cosmetic Techniques & Management at Seneca College followed by Hairstyling at Marca College to take her skills to the next level.

Now specializing in Bridal Makeup Artistry, Skincare Consultation and Hair Design, Michelle is able to bring her vision to life. The innovators of the Pan-Macedonian Fashion Show, Seneca's Fashion show and Red Carpet events such as The Scarlett Ball have taken pleasure in sharing knowledge with her and using Michelle as the Makeup Artist for their events. Her precision and versatility in Hair Design has been featured in theater shows such as 'Lullaby of Broadway' and 'Harvey' presented by the Toronto Catholic District School Board Staff Arts; established companies like Brown Man Clothing Co. and for upcoming musical artist, Champagne. The way she naturally levels with her clients allows her to easily collaborate knowledge and ideas, bringing their vision to life with a flare!

"Pushing boundaries, never compromising beauty and style." -Michelle Patricio
